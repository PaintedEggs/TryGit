Edward Thomas "Tom" Hardy (born 15 September 1977) is an English actor. His most notable film roles include Star Trek: Nemesis, RocknRolla, Bronson, Inception (for which he won the BAFTA Rising Star Award), Warrior, Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy, This Means War, The Dark Knight Rises (in which he played Bane), and Lawless.
Hardy's television roles include the BBC miniseries The Virgin Queen, ITV's Wuthering Heights (in which he played Heathcliff), Stuart: A Life Backwards, and Martina Cole's The Take. In 2014, he will star as Max Rockatansky in Mad Max: Fury Road, the fourth instalment of the Mad Max franchise.
