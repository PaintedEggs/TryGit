Guy Edward Pearce (born 5 October 1967) is an English-born Australian actor and musician, mainly known for his roles as Mike Young in the Australian television series Neighbours and films such as The Adventures of Priscilla, Queen of the Desert (1994), L.A. Confidential (1997), Memento (2000), The King's Speech (2010), Lockout (2012), Prometheus (2012), and Iron Man 3 (2013). He has won an Emmy Award and received nominations for Golden Globe Awards, Screen Actors Guild Awards, and Saturn Awards.
